# STAR TREK: THE LONG GAME
## Scene 5B — "THE CALL"

---

`INT. ENTERPRISE-A — CAPTAIN'S READY ROOM — LATER`

Kirk alone.

The communications channel is open. The screen shows ADMIRAL VOSS.

She is sitting very straight. She has been sitting very straight since before the channel opened.

She has reviewed the tactical record.

She doesn't raise her voice.

---

**VOSS**
The Emergency Disclosure window was open for thirty-eight seconds.

Kirk says nothing.

---

**VOSS** *(CONT'D)*
Your tactical officer had a valid sensor reading at the fourteen-second mark. Sufficient cause to invoke the protocol existed at the twenty-second mark. At the absolute latest.

*(beat)*

He invoked nothing. He said nothing. The window closed.

---

**KIRK**
The reading was fragmentary, Admiral. My officer was being thorough. The protocol carries significant diplomatic consequences if invoked without —

---

**VOSS**
Captain.

He stops.

---

**VOSS** *(CONT'D)*
We are now in a position where we are making accusations we cannot prove to people who are very good at appearing reasonable.

*(beat)*

That position has a name. It's called losing.

A silence.

---

**VOSS** *(CONT'D)*
Get your bridge in order.

She closes the channel.

Kirk sits with it.

He sits with it for exactly long enough.

Then he stands.

Goes to find Hummer.

`CUT TO:`

---

*End of Scene 5B*
